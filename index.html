Immerse yourself in a world where stories shape realities, words build bridges, and culture finds its true voice.

Founded in 2015 under the visionary mentorship of Nitin Bhatnagar Sir, Club Literario has blossomed into a dynamic platform where literature, culture, and entertainment come together to inspire, connect, and transform lives.

Club Literario is more than a literary group—it's a movement. We celebrate creativity through intimate book discussions, cultural fests, and unforgettable events like DJ nights. Our workshops sharpen skills in poetry and prose, while author meet-ups and engaging experiences open doors to new perspectives in literature, arts, and entertainment.
